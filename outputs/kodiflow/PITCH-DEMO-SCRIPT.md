# KodiFlow — 5-minute demo script

## Opening (30 seconds)

“Most independent landlords still reconcile rent from M-Pesa messages and
spreadsheets. KodiFlow gives them one live view of properties, tenants, rent,
utilities, arrears, and expenses—while payments go directly to each landlord’s
own Paybill or Till.”

## 1. Portfolio overview (45 seconds)

Enter the **Landlord demo** and show the dashboard:

- Total rent collected
- Outstanding balances
- Occupancy
- Recent M-Pesa payments

Say: “The landlord knows what was paid, what is late, and which units are vacant
without calling a caretaker.”

## 2. Tenant payment identity (45 seconds)

Open **Tenants**, then **Units**.

Say: “Every occupied unit receives a unique KodiFlow account reference. Tenants
use that reference when paying, allowing automatic matching even across several
properties.”

## 3. M-Pesa moment (90 seconds)

Open **Invoices** and click **Demo payment** on an outstanding invoice.

Pause while the simulated STK sequence runs. Then show **Payments**.

Say: “KodiFlow sends the prompt, receives Safaricom’s callback, matches the
account reference, clears the oldest invoice, updates arrears, and issues a
receipt—without the landlord touching a spreadsheet.”

Show the unmatched-payment section if present:

“If a tenant enters the wrong account reference, the money is never lost. It
lands in this reconciliation queue for the landlord to match manually.”

## 4. Operations (45 seconds)

Open **Utilities**, **Expenses**, and **Reminders**.

Say: “Rent is only part of property management. KodiFlow bills utilities,
records property expenses, and prepares targeted SMS or WhatsApp follow-ups.”

## 5. Platform opportunity (45 seconds)

Exit the demo and enter **Platform admin demo**.

Say: “KodiFlow is multi-landlord software. From here, the platform operator can
manage landlord accounts, subscriptions, portfolio growth, and payment setup.”

## Close (30 seconds)

“KodiFlow helps landlords collect faster, save time, and understand their
properties. We’re looking for pilot landlords to validate the workflow with
real portfolios and sandbox M-Pesa integration.”

Ask: “Would you be open to piloting KodiFlow with one property for 30 days?”
